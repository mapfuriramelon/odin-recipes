Yummy Baked Potatoes

Try this recipe of ours for yummy and delicious baked potatoes you have ever tasted and you will surely come back for more

Description

What a way to start your evening family time with a dinner consisting of baked potatoes made from our recipe

This recipe is inherited from mygreat-grandfather who was passionate about potatoes and taught me to make great recipes at a tender age of seven. And, baked potatoes were one of the first recipes i learnt from my dearly beloved oupa.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 4 medium russet potatoes
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • ¼ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • Sea salt
  • Tempeh Bacon Bits
  • Chives
  • Cashew Sour Cream

    • Steps

    1. Preheat the oven to 425°F and line two baking sheet with parchment paper.
    2. AUse a fork to poke a few holes into the potatoes. Place on the baking sheet, rub with olive oil, and sprinkle liberally with sea salt all over. Bake 45 to 60 minutes, or until the potato is fork-tender and the skin is crisp
    3. Slice open each potato. Assemble with a dollop of sour cream or cashew cream, tempeh bacon bits, and a sprinkle of chives. Serve with remaining cashew cream and tempeh bacon on the side.
    4. Place the potato in the preheated oven, and bake until slightly soft and golden brown, about 90 minutes. Slice the potato down the center, and serve with butter and black pepper. Sprinkle shredded Cheddar cheese over the top, if desired.
